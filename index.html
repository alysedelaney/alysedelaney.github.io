How to Make it:

To start, you will need to buy a box of mac and cheese. Be sure to get some milk and butter as well, as it makes it so much better!

Steps to take:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil over high.
  2. Once boiling, add noodles to the pot. Cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Turn off stove. Drain noodles and return to pot.
  4. Add cheese powder, milk, butter, and stir.
  5. Enjoy!

You can also add various extras to mac and cheese. I like green peas and hot sauce. Some people like hot dogs.
